Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for both Nintendo Homebrew and the Kurisu moderation bot

NH and Kurisu use and collect your data in the following ways:

  1. We log changes to your public Discord profile, such as username changes.
  2. We store your messages, which are encrypted server side, to enable us to provide accurate moderation and handle restriction and ban appeals.
  3. You are informed of this during server onboarding, which you must read to interact with the server.

If you do not consent to your data being used in this way, please leave the server and do not interact with it.

NH and Kurisu do NOT and will not ever:

  1. Sell your data.
  2. Scrape, modify or transform your data.
  3. Use your data to advertise to you.
  4. Use your data to train artificial intelligence, generative language models or any other similar activities.
  5. Use your data in relation to any other Discord server, organisation or team, or generally for any other purpose other than described herein.

If you would like your data to be deleted, please send an email to Email staff
